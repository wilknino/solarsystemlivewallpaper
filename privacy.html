Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025 · Effective immediately upon app installation

🛡️

Short version: Solar System Live Wallpaper collects absolutely no personal data. The app runs entirely on your device, requires no account, sends nothing to any server, and never has and never will sell or share your information.

1. Who We Are

Solar System Live Wallpaper ("the App", "we", "our", "us") is an Android live wallpaper application developed and published as a paid app on the Google Play Store. For support or privacy inquiries, contact us at spreadlov.aid@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information whatsoever. The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, provide your name, email address, location, phone number, or any other personal identifier.

Specifically, the App does not collect, store, transmit, or process:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, device serial number)
  • Location data (GPS, IP-based, or otherwise)
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports sent to external servers
  • Photos, contacts, calendar data, or any other device content
  • Browsing history or app usage patterns

3. How the App Works

All computation in the App — orbital mechanics, planet positions, eclipse calculations, 3D rendering — happens entirely on your device. The App:

  • Requires no internet connection to function
  • Does not make any network requests
  • Stores only your preference settings (simulation speed, display scale, chosen date, etc.) locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences
  • Never sends these preferences or any other data off-device

Your locally stored preferences remain on your device and are deleted when you uninstall the App.

4. Android Permissions

The App requests only the permissions necessary to function as an Android Live Wallpaper:

  • BIND_WALLPAPER — required by Android for all live wallpaper apps to attach to the home screen
  • SET_WALLPAPER — allows the in-app "Set Wallpaper" button to work

No permissions related to location, contacts, camera, microphone, storage, or network access are requested.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social media SDKs.

The App is distributed through Google Play. Google may collect information about your purchase and device in accordance with their own Privacy Policy (policies.google.com/privacy). We have no control over and receive no data from Google's processes.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not target children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children. Because the App collects no personal information from anyone, it is inherently safe for users of all ages. Parents and guardians can allow children to use the App with confidence.

7. Data Security

Because we collect no personal data, there is no personal data to secure or protect on our end. Your locally stored preferences are protected by Android's standard app sandboxing — other apps cannot access them.

8. Data Retention

We retain no data on any server because we collect no data. Local preferences stored on your device are retained until you clear the App's data or uninstall it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights under laws such as GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), or similar privacy regulations. Because we collect no personal data, there is no data to access, export, correct, or delete on our side. If you have any privacy-related questions, please contact us at the address below.

We do not anticipate changes to this policy, as our commitment to zero data collection is fundamental to the App's design.
